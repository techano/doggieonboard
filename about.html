Meet the Passionate Minds Behind Doggie on Board

At Doggie on Board, we're powered by a group of dedicated college students who share a deep love for pets. As a collective, we bring together a range of unique skills and talents to make car rides safer and more enjoyable for your four-legged friends. Each face in our team represents a different facet of our mission – from visionaries to creatives and makers. Scroll down to meet our talented crew and discover how their expertise is shaping the future of pet travel. Join us on this exciting journey, as our family of pet lovers works to redefine pet safety and style.

Derek - The Visionary Leader

Derek leads the pack, steering Doggie on Board with his vision and unbridled enthusiasm. A college student with a passion for pet safety, he's the driving force behind our adventure.
